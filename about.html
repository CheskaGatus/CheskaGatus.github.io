About

Data Analyst with Bachelor of Science in Commerce, major in International Business and Economics, cum laude. More than 10 years of experience in commercial and specialty light fixtures manufacturing industry and international airline cargo operations involving varied clients’ requirements and expectations. A consistent top performer with proven competence in adapting to change and multitasking challenges. Solid organizational skills and extreme attention to detail led to proficiency in Git, MySQL and Python Programming. Strong work ethics, great interpersonal relations, and superior communication abilities in working with peers and management, as well as dealing with clients. Extensive experience in customers’ needs assessment, product evaluation, pricing analysis, data analytics and visualization contributed to management recognition on work excellence.
